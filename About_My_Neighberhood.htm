Dherkeynley District
Suuq Yaraha
Suuq Yaraha is a lively and growing neighborhood located in the heart of Dherkenley District, Mogadishu. Known for its strong sense of community, this area blends traditional Somali life with modern development.

The streets are always filled with movement — from children playing to families shopping and neighbors chatting. It’s a place where people know each other, help one another, and share both joy and challenges together.

Religious and cultural life is strong here. The mosques are always active, especially during prayer times and Islamic events, bringing the community closer with shared values and traditions.
My Neighbourhood
Community & Lifestyle
Suuq Yaraha is located in Dherkenley District, within the Xanaano Bulsho sector.  I’ve lived here for over 13 years, since the age of 6.  The area is peaceful, welcoming, and socially active.  I completed my early education at Zubeir School — one of the top schools in the neighborhood — along with other respected institutions like Maxamuud Ahmed Ali and Amoud Schools.
Culture & Religion
Masjid Taqwo is one of the most special places to me — it was the first mosque I prayed in after moving to Suuq Yaraha, and I still go there regularly.  A big part of my Qur’an studies also took place right here in the neighborhood.  The people are cooperative, peaceful, and united in faith and community spirit.
Business & Local Markets
Suuq Yaraha thrives with business and daily trading.  The local market is full of energy and offers everything from clothes and vegetables to electronics.  Entrepreneurship and commerce are a strong part of the area's identity and everyday life.
