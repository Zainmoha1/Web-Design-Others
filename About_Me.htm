Khalid Mohamed Farah
Graphic Designer
I'm Khalid Mohamed Farah, a passionate creative and computer science student with a strong foundation in technology and design. Born and raised in Hodan District, Mogadishu, I have always been driven by curiosity and a desire to grow.

I graduated from Zubeir School in Dherkenley after completing my primary to secondary education in the 2022–2023 academic year.

Currently, I'm studying Computer Science at the Somali National University – Geheyr Campus, under the Faculty of Science.

Alongside my academic path, I studied Graphic Design at the Somali Digital Media Academy (SODMA), which helped me sharpen my creative skills and deepen my understanding of visual communication.
